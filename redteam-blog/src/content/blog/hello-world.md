Writing something up is the fastest way to find out whether I actually understand it. That's most of the reason this site exists. The rest: almost everything I know came from someone else's write-up, and a public record of work beats a CV.

What to expect:

- Lab write-ups — Active Directory, cloud, infrastructure
- Methodology and tooling notes
- Short opinions, occasionally

Ground rules: everything published here is from a lab environment or sanitised and shared with permission. Nothing operational, nothing attributable to a client.
